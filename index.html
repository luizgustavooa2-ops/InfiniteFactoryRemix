Welcome to Infinite Factory!

Infinite Factory is a game where you build an unlimited variety of machines that produce an unlimited variety of resources.

Stats

Stats are shown in the top panel. They are consumed and produced by machines every second.

Machines

Machines manipulate stats. A copper miner might consume 1 energy and produce 1 copper per second. You can disable or delete any machine. If you need a copper miner but it requires copper to build, make the basic version first — basic machines never require the resource they produce.

Inverted Stats

Some stats are Inverted (like Server Load or Heat). They don't accumulate — they show an instantaneous balance. If the balance exceeds 0 the stat is OVERLOADED and all machines producing it stall until you add machines that consume it.

Analyse Factory

Click Analyse Factory to get an AI summary of your factory and improvement suggestions. Past analyses appear in Factory History.
